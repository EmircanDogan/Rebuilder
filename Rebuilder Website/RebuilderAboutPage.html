Rebuilder

What is our mission?

Our Mission: Starting a Transformation Movement Today, we focus on an innovative recycling system we call Rebuilder, in order to raise awareness about environmental problems, waste management and sustainability and to initiate a change movement in society. Our project offers a unique solution to reduce environmental impacts by using plastic bottles instead of bricks instead of throwing them away. Our Vision: Environmental Awareness and Social Participation In addition to reducing environmental impacts by utilizing plastic bottles, Rebuilder aims to increase social participation and encourage sustainable habits. In line with this vision, we aim to offer various advantages to users by collaborating with brands. Our Incentive System: Earn Points from Pet Bottles, Make a Difference in Sustainability! The basis of our project is a scoring system that provides incentives to every individual who evaluates plastic bottles. Users can save each pet bottle to our system by scanning a special barcode. Each barcode scanned will earn the user points, and these points will be rewarded with shopping discounts or gift certificates from various brands.
